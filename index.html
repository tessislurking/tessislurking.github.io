About the show

On A Whim is a biweekly internet radio show brought to you by Aberdeen Student Radio. There is no set theme so every week there is a new and exciting topic to be discussed! It's hosted by Tess Schulze, a Second Year studying English and Philosophy at the University of Aberdeen. On A Whim recently won the Best Talk Show Award for ASR! Tune in to find out why!
